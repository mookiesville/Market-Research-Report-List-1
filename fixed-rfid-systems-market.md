<p><strong>The Fixed RFID Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LF RFID System</li><li>HF RFID System</li><li>UHF RFID System</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fixed RFID systems are categorized into several market types based on frequency ranges. Low Frequency (LF) RFID systems operate at 125-134 kHz, suitable for animal tracking and access control due to their short read range and ability to work in challenging environments. High Frequency (HF) RFID systems, operating at 13.56 MHz, are used in library systems and payment applications for their moderate range. Ultra-High Frequency (UHF) RFID systems, ranging from 300 MHz to 3 GHz, enable long-range tracking and high-speed inventory management. Other market types may include specialized applications like active RFID systems, which use battery-powered tags for real-time monitoring.</p></p>
