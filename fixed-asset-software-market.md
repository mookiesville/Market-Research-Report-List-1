<p><p>Fixed asset software can be categorized into three primary market types: on-premise, web-based, and cloud-based. On-premise solutions are installed locally on a company's servers, offering greater control and security but requiring significant IT resources. Web-based software operates through internet browsers, providing accessibility from any device, but may raise concerns regarding data security. Cloud-based solutions store data on external servers, ensuring easy scalability and remote access while minimizing upfront costs and maintenance responsibilities for users.</p></p>
