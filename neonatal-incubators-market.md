<p><p>The neonatal incubator market comprises two primary types: transport neonatal incubators and normal neonatal incubators. Transport neonatal incubators are specialized devices designed for the safe and controlled transport of vulnerable newborns, providing temperature regulation and essential monitoring during transfers. Normal neonatal incubators, on the other hand, are stationary units used within hospitals to maintain optimal thermal conditions and protect premature infants from environmental hazards while ensuring access for medical staff. Both types play crucial roles in neonatal care.</p></p>
