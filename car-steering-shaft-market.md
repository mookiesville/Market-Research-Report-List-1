<p><strong>The Car Steering Shaft Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rack and Pinion Type</li><li>Worm Crank Pin Type</li><li>Recirculating Ball Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car steering shaft market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Rack and Pinion Type** – This design offers direct steering response through a gear mechanism that translates the rotational movement of the steering wheel into linear motion, providing enhanced handling.</p><p>2. **Worm Crank Pin Type** – This older system features a worm gear and a crank pin mechanism, delivering smooth steering but with less responsiveness compared to modern systems.</p><p>3. **Recirculating Ball Type** – This variant uses ball bearings within a nut and a worm gear, distributing load effectively, but can be bulkier, often found in heavier vehicles, offering good durability and stability.</p></p>
