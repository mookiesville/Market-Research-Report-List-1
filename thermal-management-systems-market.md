<p><ul><li>Air Cycle Refrigeration Technology</li><li>Vapor Cycle Refrigeration Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal management systems market includes two primary technologies: Air Cycle Refrigeration Technology and Vapor Cycle Refrigeration Technology. Air cycle refrigeration uses air as the working fluid, ideal for applications requiring lightweight, efficient cooling, often seen in aerospace. In contrast, vapor cycle refrigeration employs refrigerants that undergo phase changes, providing effective thermal regulation in various industries, including automotive and HVAC. Both technologies focus on maintaining optimal temperatures, enhancing system performance, and improving energy efficiency across applications.</p></p>
