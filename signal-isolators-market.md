Two-wire isolators are compact and power-efficient, ideal for loop-powered systems. Three-wire isolators require an external power supply and are used for more complex signal processing. Four-wire isolators offer higher accuracy and isolation, suitable for demanding applications. Each type caters to specific needs, balancing power requirements, installation complexity, and performance in industrial automation and control systems.</p></p>
