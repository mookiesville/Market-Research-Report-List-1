<p><strong>The Natural Graphite for Lithium Batteries Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Crystalline Graphite</li><li>Implicit Crystalline Graphite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Natural graphite for lithium batteries is categorized primarily into crystalline graphite and implicit crystalline graphite. Crystalline graphite, known for its layered structure, offers excellent electrical conductivity and is commonly used as an anode material, enhancing battery performance. Implicit crystalline graphite, on the other hand, refers to graphite that may contain certain structural imperfections or variations in purity, affecting its electrochemical properties. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing lithium-ion battery efficiency, energy density, and overall lifespan.</p></p>
