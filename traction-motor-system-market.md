<p><strong>The Traction Motor System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AC</li><li>DC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The traction motor system market is divided into two primary types: AC (Alternating Current) and DC (Direct Current). AC traction motors are widely employed in modern rail systems due to their efficiency, reliability, and ability to provide greater torque at high speeds. They are favored for electric trains and urban transit. Conversely, DC traction motors are traditionally used in older train systems, offering simplicity and ease of control, particularly in low-speed applications. Each type plays a crucial role in the development of electric transportation solutions.</p></p>
