<p><p>The Ceramic Blast Media market consists of two primary types: Ceramic Sand and Ceramic Shot. Ceramic Sand is composed of fine, granular particles used for surface preparation and finishing in industrial applications, offering low dust generation and high durability. Ceramic Shot, on the other hand, consists of spherical particles that provide efficient cleaning and peening, enhancing surface hardness and fatigue resistance. Both types are environmentally friendly alternatives to traditional abrasives, gaining popularity across various industries for their performance and reduced environmental impact.</p></p>
