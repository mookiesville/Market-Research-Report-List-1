<p><p>The ultrasonic transmitter market can be categorized into several types, including Doppler type, sound field type, and others. Doppler type transmitters utilize the Doppler effect to measure the frequency shift of reflected sound waves, making them ideal for motion detection and flow measurement. Sound field type transmitters emit ultrasonic waves in a defined area, primarily for applications like distance measurement and obstacle detection. Other types may include specialized transmitters designed for unique applications, contributing to diverse market needs and advancements.</p></p>
