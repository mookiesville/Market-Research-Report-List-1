<p><strong>The Vaginal Panel Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Molecular DNA Probe</li><li>Molecular Nucleic Acid Amplification Test (NAAT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vaginal panel testing market primarily consists of two types: Direct Molecular DNA Probe and Molecular Nucleic Acid Amplification Test (NAAT). The Direct Molecular DNA Probe involves the use of specific probes to detect the presence of pathogens directly from vaginal samples, providing quick results. In contrast, NAAT amplifies nucleic acid from pathogens, allowing for highly sensitive and specific detection, even in low concentrations. Both methods are critical for diagnosing infections and guiding appropriate treatment strategies in women's health.</p></p>
