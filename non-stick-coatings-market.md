<p><p>The non-stick coatings market primarily includes PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene), PFA (perfluoroalkoxy), and FEP (fluorinated ethylene propylene). PTFE is widely used for cookware due to its excellent non-stick properties and thermal resistance. PFA offers enhanced chemical resistance and is suitable for applications requiring flexibility and high temperatures. FEP, while similar to PTFE, features better clarity and processing characteristics, making it ideal for specific industrial applications. Each type serves distinct markets, catering to various consumer and industrial needs.</p></p>
