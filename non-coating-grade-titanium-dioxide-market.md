<p><p>The Non-coating Grade Titanium Dioxide market is divided into two primary types based on production methods: Sulfate Process and Chloride Process. The Sulfate Process involves treating titanium ore with sulfuric acid to extract titanium dioxide, resulting in a less pure product but lower production costs. In contrast, the Chloride Process uses chlorine and produces a purer titanium dioxide with superior brightness and opacity, making it more suitable for specialized applications. Each method caters to different market demands and cost considerations.</p></p>
