<p><p>The Operational Database Management System (ODMS) market comprises two primary deployment types: Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) and On-Premise solutions. SaaS offers cloud-based access, allowing users to utilize database services without the need for physical infrastructure, promoting scalability and cost-efficiency.
