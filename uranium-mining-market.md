<p><p>Uranium mining involves extracting uranium from various deposit types. Granite-Type Uranium Deposits are found in granitic rocks, often associated with hydrothermal activity. Volcanic-Type Uranium Deposits occur in volcanic rocks, formed through volcanic processes and hydrothermal alteration. Sandstone-Type Uranium Deposits are hosted in sedimentary sandstone layers, typically formed by groundwater movement. Carbonate-Siliceous-Pelitic Rock Type Uranium Deposits are found in mixed sedimentary rocks, often influenced by organic matter and redox conditions. Each type has unique geological characteristics, influencing mining methods and market dynamics.</p></p>
