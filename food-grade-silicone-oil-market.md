<p><p>The Food Grade Silicone Oil market is typically categorized into three types based on viscosity: H1, H2, and H3. H1 silicone oil serves as a food-safe lubricant for direct food contact applications, meeting stringent safety standards. H2 silicone oil has a higher viscosity, often used in processing equipment where indirect food contact occurs. H3 is characterized by even higher viscosity levels, typically utilized for specialized applications in food production, such as high-temperature environments or specialized machinery lubrication.</p></p>
