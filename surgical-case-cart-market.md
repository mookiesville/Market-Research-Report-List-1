<p><p>The surgical case cart market is divided into two main types: open case carts and closed case carts. Open case carts are typically used for easy access to surgical instruments and supplies during procedures, promoting efficiency and visibility. In contrast, closed case carts provide a secure and hygienic environment for storing instruments, minimizing contamination risk. Both types cater to different surgical environments, with open carts favored in fast-paced settings and closed carts preferred in sterile or high-infection-risk scenarios.</p></p>
