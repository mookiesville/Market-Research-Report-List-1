<p><p>Building Information Modeling (BIM) software is available in two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based BIM software allows users to access and collaborate on projects from anywhere with internet connectivity, facilitating real-time updates and teamwork. On-premise BIM software, on the other hand, is installed locally on individual systems, offering enhanced data control and security but limiting accessibility.
