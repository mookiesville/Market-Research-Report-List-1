<p><ul><li>Single Clad Fiber</li><li>Double Clad Fiber</li><li>Triple Clad Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ho-doped fibers are classified into three main types based on their cladding structure: single clad, double clad, and triple clad fibers. Single clad fibers have one layer of cladding around the core, which allows for basic light transmission. Double clad fibers feature an additional outer cladding, enhancing efficiency and reducing losses for high-power applications. Triple clad fibers provide the greatest performance by incorporating multiple cladding layers, optimizing light confinement and minimizing pump signal losses, making them ideal for advanced laser systems and telecommunications.</p></p>
