<p><p>The matte coated paper market is divided into two main types: single-sided coated paper and double-sided coated paper. Single-sided coated paper features a matte finish on one side, making it ideal for applications like invitations or covers where a smooth surface is needed for printing. Double-sided coated paper, on the other hand, offers a matte texture on both sides, making it suitable for brochures, catalogs, and other materials that require high-quality printing on both surfaces, enhancing visual appeal and print versatility.</p></p>
