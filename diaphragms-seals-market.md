<p><p>Diaphragm seals are critical components in pressure measurement systems, designed to isolate the measuring instrument from the process medium. The low-pressure diaphragm seal is engineered for applications with pressures typically in the range of 0 to 100 psi, providing accurate readings while safeguarding sensitive instruments from contamination. In contrast, high-pressure diaphragm seals are designed for environments exceeding 100 psi, ensuring reliable performance under extreme conditions, such as in oil and gas, chemical processing, and power generation industries.</p></p>
