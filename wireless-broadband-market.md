<p><strong>The Wireless Broadband Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Point-to-Point (PTP)</li><li>Point-to-Multipoint (PMP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless broadband market is divided into two main types: Point-to-Point (PTP) and Point-to-Multipoint (PMP). PTP systems establish a direct connection between two locations, providing high-speed data transmission and minimizing interference, making them ideal for backhaul applications and interconnecting networks. Conversely, PMP systems connect a single base station to multiple user devices, enabling broader coverage and flexibility for areas with various users, such as residential neighborhoods or public hotspots. Both types cater to different connectivity needs and geographical challenges.</p></p>
