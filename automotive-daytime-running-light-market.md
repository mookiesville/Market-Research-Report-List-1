<p><p>The Automotive Daytime Running Light (DRL) market includes Full-voltage and Parking light types. Full-voltage DRLs operate at the vehicle's full electrical system voltage, providing bright, energy-efficient illumination for enhanced daytime visibility and safety. They are commonly integrated into modern vehicles. Parking light DRLs, on the other hand, use lower voltage and are designed to function as both DRLs and parking lights, offering dual functionality. They are often found in older or budget-friendly models, balancing cost and performance for basic lighting needs.</p></p>
