Reflection gratings utilize light incidents on a surface and reflect it, making them ideal for applications like lasers and optical systems. Conversely, transmission gratings allow light to pass through, suitable for spectroscopy and imaging devices. Both types are essential in manipulating light for various technological applications, including telecommunications, sensors, and scientific research, by enhancing efficiency and precision in light management.</p></p>
