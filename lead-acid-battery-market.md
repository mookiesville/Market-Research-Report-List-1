<p><ul><li>VRLA Battery</li><li>Flooded Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lead-acid battery market primarily consists of two types: Valve-Regulated Lead-Acid (VRLA) batteries and flooded batteries. VRLA batteries are sealed, maintenance-free, and suitable for applications requiring a compact design, making them ideal for backup power in telecommunications and renewable energy systems. Flooded batteries, on the other hand, are open-cell designs requiring regular maintenance and electrolyte levels monitoring. They are commonly used in heavy-duty applications like forklifts and grid storage due to their robustness and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
