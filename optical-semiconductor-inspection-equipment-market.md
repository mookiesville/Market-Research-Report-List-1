<p><p>The optical semiconductor inspection equipment market features various techniques for assessing the quality of semiconductor materials. Dark field inspection employs oblique lighting to enhance surface defects against a dark background, making it effective for detecting minute imperfections. In contrast, bright field inspection utilizes direct illumination, providing a clear view of the entire sample by highlighting both defects and surface features. Each method serves specific applications, ensuring thorough quality control in semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
