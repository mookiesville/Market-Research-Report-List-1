<p><p>The grease fittings market is segmented into three main types based on their angles: straight, 45-degree, and 90-degree. The 45-degree fittings facilitate connection at an angle, accommodating slightly restricted areas while providing good accessibility. Conversely, 90-degree fittings are designed for corner applications, enabling lubrication in compact or hard-to-reach spots. Each type is essential for effective maintenance of machinery, ensuring optimal performance and longevity.</p></p>
