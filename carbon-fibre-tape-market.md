<p><p>The Carbon Fibre Tape market includes Prepreg Tape and Dry Tape. Prepreg Tape consists of carbon fibres pre-impregnated with resin, offering high strength, durability, and ease of use in manufacturing processes like aerospace and automotive. It requires curing under heat and pressure. Dry Tape, on the other hand, contains only carbon fibres without resin, providing flexibility for custom resin applications. It is often used in industries requiring tailored solutions, such as construction and sports equipment, where specific resin systems are preferred.</p></p>
