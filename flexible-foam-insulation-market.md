<p><p>Flexible foam insulation is categorized based on material types, each offering unique properties. **NBR-based** foam is known for its excellent oil and fuel resistance, making it ideal for automotive and industrial applications. **EPDM-based** foam excels in weather resistance, UV stability, and thermal performance, suitable for outdoor and HVAC systems. **Chloroprene-based** foam provides superior fire resistance and durability, often used in construction and marine applications. **Other** types include blends or specialty foams tailored for specific needs, such as acoustic or thermal insulation in niche industries.</p></p>
