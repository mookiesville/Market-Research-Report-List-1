<p><p>The Lightning Protection System for buildings encompasses two main market types: Conventional and Non-Conventional. Conventional systems utilize physical structures like rods and conductors to intercept and redirect lightning strikes safely to the ground. In contrast, Non-Conventional systems employ advanced technologies, such as charge dissipation methods and electronic devices, to prevent lightning strikes or mitigate their effects. Both systems aim to protect structures from lightning damage, but they differ in their approach and the technologies utilized for safety enhancement.</p></p>
