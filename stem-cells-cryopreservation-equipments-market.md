<p><p>Stem cell cryopreservation equipment is crucial for preserving stem cells at ultra-low temperatures to maintain their viability for future use. The market is divided into two main types: liquid phase and vapor phase. Liquid phase cryopreservation involves direct immersion of samples in liquid nitrogen, ensuring immediate cooling and effective preservation. In contrast, vapor phase cryopreservation utilizes the nitrogen vapor above the liquid, providing a safer storage environment with reduced contamination risks. Both methods are essential for successful stem cell storage and research applications.</p></p>
