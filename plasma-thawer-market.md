<p><ul><li>Wet Plasma Thawer</li><li>Dry Plasma Thawer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Plasma Thawer market consists of two primary types: Wet Plasma Thawers and Dry Plasma Thawers. Wet Plasma Thawers utilize water baths to gently thaw frozen plasma, ensuring minimal disruption to its components, often preferred for rapid processing in clinical settings. In contrast, Dry Plasma Thawers employ air or indirect heat methods, allowing for a more controlled thawing environment, which reduces the risk of contamination. Both types cater to different laboratory needs, emphasizing efficiency and safety in blood product handling.</p></p>
