<p><p>Glass door freezers are categorized into two main types: hinged door and sliding door. Hinged door freezers feature a traditional design with doors that swing open, providing easy access but requiring more space for operation. Conversely, sliding door freezers have doors that move horizontally, allowing for space-saving access, making them ideal for tight retail environments. Both types enhance product visibility while maintaining temperature control, catering to diverse consumer preferences in retail and food service settings.</p></p>
