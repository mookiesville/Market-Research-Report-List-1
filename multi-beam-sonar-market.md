<p><p>The multi-beam sonar market is categorized into low frequency, medium frequency, and high frequency types based on operational range and application. Low frequency sonar, typically below 1 kHz, is ideal for long-range detection in deep waters, making it suitable for naval applications and large-scale surveys. Medium frequency sonar operates between 1 kHz and 10 kHz, balancing range and resolution, often used in fishing and marine research. High frequency sonar, above 10 kHz, provides high-resolution imaging for shallow waters, benefiting underwater construction and detailed mapping.</p></p>
