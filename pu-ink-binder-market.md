<p><p>The PU ink binder market encompasses two primary types: water-based and solvent-based inks. Water-based PU ink binders are formulated with polymers that dissolve in water, offering eco-friendliness and ease of cleanup, making them suitable for various applications like textiles and packaging. In contrast, solvent-based PU ink binders utilize organic solvents, providing excellent adhesion and durability, which is ideal for high-performance applications such as automotive coatings and industrial printing. Both types cater to specific industry needs for flexibility, performance, and environmental compliance.</p></p>
