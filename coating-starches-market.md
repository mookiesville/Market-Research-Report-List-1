Cross-linked starch offers improved resistance to heat and shear, making it ideal for industrial applications. Hydroxyalkyl starch provides solubility and thickening properties, often utilized in cosmetics and personal care products. Porous starch serves as an effective carrier for flavors and nutrients in food products, enhancing texture and delivering controlled release.</p></p>
