<p><p>The seedling substrate market comprises various types, including soilless mixes, composts, gravel, inert media, and others. Soilless mixes offer a lightweight and nutrient-rich environment for seedlings. Composts provide organic matter, enhancing soil fertility. Gravel serves as a drainage solution, promoting root health. Inert media, like perlite or vermiculite, ensures proper aeration and moisture retention. Other substrates may include specialized blends tailored for specific plants or growth conditions, catering to diverse horticultural needs and preferences in seedling cultivation.</p></p>
