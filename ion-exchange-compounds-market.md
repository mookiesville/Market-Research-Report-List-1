<p><p>The ion exchange compounds market comprises two primary types: anion exchange compounds and cation exchange compounds. Anion exchange compounds facilitate the removal of negatively charged ions from solutions, commonly used in water treatment and purification processes. Conversely, cation exchange compounds target positively charged ions, serving applications in pharmaceuticals, fertilizers, and industrial processes. Both types are crucial for maintaining water quality, enhancing chemical processes, and ensuring environmental safety by selectively removing unwanted ions from various solutions.</p></p>
