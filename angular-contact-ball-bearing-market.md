<p><p>Angular contact ball bearings are categorized based on their contact angle, which affects their load capacity and performance characteristics. The common types include 15°, 25°, 30°, and 40° contact angles. A 15° angle offers low axial load capacity, suitable for high-speed applications. A 25° angle balances axial and radial loads, making it versatile. The 30° and 40° angles provide higher axial load capacities, ideal for heavy-duty applications. "Other" covers specialized designs for unique requirements, adapting to various operational demands.</p></p>
