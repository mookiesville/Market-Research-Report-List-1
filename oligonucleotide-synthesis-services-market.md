<p><ul><li>Primers</li><li>Probes</li><li>Intermediate-scale Synthesis Oligos</li><li>Large-scale Synthesis Oligos</li><li>Linkers and Adaptors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oligonucleotide synthesis services encompass various market types catering to diverse research needs. Primers are short sequences used in PCR and sequencing, while probes serve as molecular tools for detection in assays. Intermediate-scale synthesis oligos are produced for moderate research applications, while large-scale synthesis oligos are designed for high-throughput projects. Linkers and adaptors facilitate the connection of different sequences in molecular cloning and library preparation. Each type plays a crucial role in biotechnology and genetic research.</p></p>
