<p><ul><li>Fine Needle Aspiration Biopsy (FNAB)</li><li>Core Needle Biopsy (CNB)</li><li>Vacuum Assisted Biopsy (VAB)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aspiration and biopsy needle market comprises three primary types: Fine Needle Aspiration Biopsy (FNAB), Core Needle Biopsy (CNB), and Vacuum Assisted Biopsy (VAB). FNAB utilizes a thin needle for sampling cells, ideal for superficial lesions. CNB employs a larger needle to extract tissue samples, providing more comprehensive histological analysis. VAB combines vacuum suction with larger gauge needles, allowing for the collection of multiple tissue samples with minimal invasiveness, making it suitable for various applications in diagnostic pathology.</p></p>
