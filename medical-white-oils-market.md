<p><p>The Medical White Oils market consists of two primary types: paraffin-based and cycloalkyl-based oils. Paraffin-based oils, derived from refined mineral oil, are known for their purity and wide applications in cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and personal care products, serving as lubricants or emollients. Cycloalkyl-based oils, featuring a cycloalkane structure, offer enhanced stability and lower viscosity. These oils are often used in specialized medical formulations and provide excellent skin compatibility, making them suitable for various therapeutic applications. Both types prioritize safety and efficacy in medical use.</p></p>
