<p><strong>The Dairy Cultures Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermophilic Type</li><li>Mesophilic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dairy cultures can be categorized into thermophilic and mesophilic types based on their optimal growth temperatures. Thermophilic cultures thrive at higher temperatures (around 45–55°C) and are typically used in the production of yogurt and certain cheeses, promoting rapid fermentation. In contrast, mesophilic cultures flourish at moderate temperatures (around 20–30°C) and are essential for making products like cream cheese and some soft cheeses, contributing to distinct flavors and textures. Both types play crucial roles in the dairy industry.</p></p>
