Screening equipment separates grains based on size and quality, ensuring uniformity and removing impurities. Drying machinery removes moisture from grains to enhance shelf life and prevent spoilage. Together, these technologies play a crucial role in the efficient processing of grains, improving product quality and maximizing yield in the agricultural sector.</p></p>
