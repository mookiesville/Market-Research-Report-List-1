<p><ul><li>Asynchronous Wind-driven Generator</li><li>Synchronous Wind-driven Generator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wind-driven generator market can be categorized into two main types: asynchronous and synchronous generators. Asynchronous wind-driven generators, commonly known as induction generators, operate independently of the grid frequency and are favored for their robustness and low maintenance costs. In contrast, synchronous wind-driven generators are grid-synchronized, enabling them to provide reactive power support, enhancing overall grid stability. Each technology has distinct advantages, appealing to different market segments based on performance, efficiency, and operational requirements.</p></p>
