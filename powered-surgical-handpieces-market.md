<p><p>The Powered Surgical Handpieces market includes three main types: electric-powered, battery-powered, and pneumatic-powered handpieces. Electric-powered handpieces provide consistent energy through direct electrical connections, offering high precision and control. Battery-powered handpieces offer portability and convenience, allowing for flexible use in various surgical scenarios without reliance on direct power sources. Pneumatic-powered handpieces utilize pressurized air, delivering powerful and rapid performance, often in high-volume applications. Each type caters to specific surgical needs, enhancing efficiency and outcomes in the operating room.</p></p>
