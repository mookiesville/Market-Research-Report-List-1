<p><p>The float type steam traps market includes several key variations. Floating ball steam traps utilize a buoyant ball to control the discharge of condensate, ensuring efficient removal. Bimetallic floating ball steam traps combine thermal expansion principles with a float mechanism, enhancing reliability in variable temperatures. Mechanical steam traps employ mechanical means to separate condensate from steam, ensuring minimal energy loss. Inverted bucket steam traps utilize a buoyant bucket that inverts to eliminate condensate, maintaining steam integrity in various applications.</p></p>
