<p><p>A ship waste compactor is a specialized device used on ships to compress and reduce the volume of waste generated onboard, such as food waste, plastics, and other non-recyclable materials. By compacting waste, these systems minimize storage space requirements, reduce disposal costs, and contribute to environmental sustainability by limiting the amount of waste dumped into oceans.</p><p>The Ship Waste Compactor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.6% during the forecast period.
