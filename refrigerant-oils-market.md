<p><p>The refrigerant oils market comprises primarily two types: mineral oils and synthetic oils. Mineral oils, derived from petroleum, are traditional and cost-effective options but have limitations in thermodynamic properties and compatibility with modern refrigerants. Synthetic oils, on the other hand, are chemically engineered to enhance performance, offering superior lubrication, stability, and lower environmental impact. They are generally more expensive but provide better efficiency and longer lifespan in refrigeration systems, catering to the growing demand for advanced cooling technologies.</p></p>
