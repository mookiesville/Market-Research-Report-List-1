<p><p>The printing inks market comprises several types, including lithographic inks, flexographic inks, digital inks, and gravure inks. Lithographic inks are used in offset printing, providing high-quality images on paper. Flexographic inks are designed for packaging and labels, known for their fast-drying properties. Digital inks cater to modern digital printing processes, allowing for quick and versatile printing on various substrates. Gravure inks are used in high-volume publications and flexible packaging, offering excellent color intensity and durability. Each type serves distinct applications and industries.</p></p>
