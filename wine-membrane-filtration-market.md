<p><p>The wine membrane filtration market includes various membrane types, each serving specific filtration needs. Polyamide (nylon) membranes offer chemical resistance, while polycarbonate and polypropylene provide cost-effective options for bulk filtration. Polytetrafluoroethylene membranes excel in high-temperature applications. Ceramic and metal membranes provide durability and high-performance filtration for harsh conditions. Molecular sieve composite membranes are designed for selective separation, and glass membranes offer fine filtration precision in wine processing.</p></p>
