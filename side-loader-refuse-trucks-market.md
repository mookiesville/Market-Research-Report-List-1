<p><p>Side loader refuse trucks are specialized vehicles designed for waste collection, featuring mechanisms to lift and empty bins from the side. The market is divided into **Automatic Side Loading (ASL)** and **Manual Side Loading (MSL)** types. ASL trucks use automated arms to lift and empty bins, reducing labor and improving efficiency. MSL trucks require manual handling by workers to load waste, making them more labor-intensive but cost-effective for smaller operations. Both types cater to diverse waste management needs, balancing automation and manual effort.</p></p>
