Two-roll machines are simple and efficient for basic sheet rolling tasks, offering high productivity for thinner materials. Three-roll machines provide enhanced versatility for various configurations, enabling the rolling of thicker sheets with better control. Four-roll machines are advanced systems, offering improved precision and the capability to produce intricate shapes and profiles, catering to complex manufacturing needs.</p></p>
