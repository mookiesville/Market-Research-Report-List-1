<p><strong>The Quiet Tire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Tire</li><li>Bias Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Quiet Tire market includes Radial and Bias Tire types. Radial Tires feature layers of fabric cords arranged at 90 degrees to the direction of travel, offering better fuel efficiency, durability, and a quieter ride due to reduced friction. Bias Tires have crisscrossed cord layers at 30-40 degree angles, providing sturdiness and load-bearing capacity but generating more noise and heat. Radial Tires dominate the market for their performance and comfort, while Bias Tires are preferred for heavy-duty applications despite their noisier operation.</p></p>
