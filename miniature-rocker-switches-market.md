<p><p>The miniature rocker switch market encompasses various types, including Single Pole Single Throw (SPST), Single Pole Double Throw (SPDT), Double Pole Single Throw (DPST), and Double Pole Double Throw (DPDT) switches. SPST switches control one circuit, while SPDT can alternate between two circuits. DPST switches control two circuits simultaneously, and DPDT allows for alternating between two circuits with dual control. Each type serves specific applications in consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial automation, catering to diverse operational needs and user preferences.</p></p>
