<p><p>The monoclonal antibody diagnostic reagents market encompasses various assay types for detecting specific antigens. Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assays (ELISA) utilize enzyme-conjugated antibodies for quantifying targets. Dot-Immunogold Filtration Assays involve colored gold particles for visual detection. The Double Antigen Sandwich Chemiluminescence Method enhances sensitivity using luminescent detection in a sandwich format. Recombinant Immunoblot Assays combine separation and detection via antibodies, providing specificity and quantitative results. Each method offers unique advantages in diagnostics, catering to different clinical needs.</p></p>
