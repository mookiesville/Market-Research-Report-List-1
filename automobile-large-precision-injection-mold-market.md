<p><p>The automobile large precision injection mold market features primarily two types: single-cavity molds and multi-cavity molds. Single-cavity molds produce one part per cycle, typically yielding higher precision and lower initial costs, making them ideal for custom or low-volume production. In contrast, multi-cavity molds can create multiple parts per cycle, enhancing efficiency and reducing unit costs in mass production. Both types cater to the demand for high-quality automotive components, contributing to advancements in manufacturing processes and product performance.</p></p>
