All Glass Vacuum Tubes consist of two glass layers, creating an efficient thermal insulation environment. Heat Pipe Vacuum Tubes use heat pipes to transfer heat quickly, making them ideal for low ambient temperatures. U-shaped Vacuum Tubes feature a U-shaped design that enhances heat collection, optimizing thermal efficiency. Each type caters to specific installation and efficiency needs.</p></p>
