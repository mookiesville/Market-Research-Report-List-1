<p><ul><li>Motor Start Capacitors</li><li>Motor Run Capacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric motor capacitors market primarily consists of two types: motor start capacitors and motor run capacitors. Motor start capacitors provide a surge of energy to initiate a motor's operation, typically used in applications like air conditioners and refrigerators. In contrast, motor run capacitors are designed to enhance efficiency during continuous operation, ensuring stable performance in various appliances such as pumps and fans. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing the functionality and reliability of electric motors across industries.</p></p>
