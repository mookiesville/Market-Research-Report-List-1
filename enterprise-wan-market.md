<p><p>The Enterprise WAN market comprises two primary types: Traditional WAN and SD-WAN. Traditional WAN relies on dedicated circuits like MPLS for consistent connectivity, offering robust performance but high costs and inflexibility. In contrast, SD-WAN utilizes software-defined networking to manage multiple connection types, optimizing performance and enhancing agility. This innovative approach reduces expenses by leveraging low-cost internet connections, improves bandwidth management, and provides better support for cloud applications, making it a more adaptable choice for modern enterprises.</p></p>
