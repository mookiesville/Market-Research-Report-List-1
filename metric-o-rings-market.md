<p><p>The Metric O-Rings market can be categorized into two main types: static seals and dynamic seals. Static seals are used in applications where components remain stationary, providing a reliable barrier against fluid leakage, often found in hydraulic and pneumatic systems. Dynamic seals, on the other hand, accommodate relative motion between parts, crucial for applications in rotating machinery, like engines and pumps. Both types play essential roles in ensuring efficiency and longevity in various industrial processes and products.</p></p>
