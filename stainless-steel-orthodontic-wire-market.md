<p><p>The stainless steel orthodontic wire market features two main types: multi-strand and single strand wire. Multi-strand stainless steel wire consists of multiple intertwined strands, offering enhanced flexibility and resilience, making it ideal for complex orthodontic adjustments. In contrast, single strand stainless steel wire is characterized by its single, solid construction, providing greater strength and stability, which is suitable for simpler orthodontic applications. Both types cater to varying clinical needs, ensuring effective treatment outcomes in orthodontics.</p></p>
