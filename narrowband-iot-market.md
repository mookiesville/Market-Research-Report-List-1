<p><p>Narrowband IoT (NB-IoT) caters to two primary market types: Short-Range Communication and Low Power Wide Area Network (LPWAN). Short-range communication involves devices transmitting data over short distances, ideal for applications like home automation and smart city infrastructure. In contrast, LPWAN offers extensive coverage with low power consumption, making it suitable for remote monitoring, agricultural sensors, and smart metering. Both types emphasize efficiency, scalability, and connectivity, enabling a wide range of IoT applications while minimizing energy use.</p></p>
