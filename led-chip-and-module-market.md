<p><ul><li>Lateral Chip</li><li>Vertical Chip</li><li>Flip Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED chip and module market features three primary types: lateral chip, vertical chip, and flip chip. Lateral chips are mounted on a substrate with light emitted sideways, offering good efficiency for low-power applications. Vertical chips provide a higher-density arrangement and improved thermal management, suitable for higher power needs. Flip chips are mounted face-down, enabling better thermal dissipation and enhanced light output, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Each type addresses specific performance, efficiency, and application requirements in the LED market.</p></p>
