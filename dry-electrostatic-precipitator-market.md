<p><p>The Dry Electrostatic Precipitator (ESP) market is primarily divided into two types: Fixed-Electrode and Moving-Electrode systems. Fixed-Electrode precipitators maintain stationary plates that collect charged particles, offering lower maintenance needs and steady performance. In contrast, Moving-Electrode systems feature electrodes that periodically change position to enhance particle collection efficiency and manage ash buildup. Both types are utilized in various industries, effectively reducing emissions and improving air quality while meeting stringent regulatory standards and operational demands.</p></p>
