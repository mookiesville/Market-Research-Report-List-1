<p><ul><li>Semi-Active Suspension System</li><li>Active Suspension System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Advanced Suspension Control System market includes Semi-Active and Active Suspension Systems. Semi-Active systems adjust damping forces in real-time using sensors and actuators, offering improved ride comfort and stability without external energy input. Active Suspension Systems use external power to control each wheel independently, providing superior handling, reduced body roll, and enhanced adaptability to road conditions. Both systems aim to optimize vehicle performance, safety, and comfort, with active systems offering higher precision at a greater cost and complexity.</p></p>
