<p><p>The plastics extrusion market encompasses various materials, each with distinct properties and applications. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is widely used for pipes and profiles due to its durability and resistance to environmental stress. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) is flexible and commonly used for packaging films and containers. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) offers greater strength and is ideal for containers, piping, and industrial applications. PS (Polystyrene) is utilized in both rigid and foam forms, suitable for packaging, insulation, and consumer products. Each type serves specific industry needs.</p></p>
