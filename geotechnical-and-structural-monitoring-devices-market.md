<p><p>The Geotechnical and Structural Monitoring Devices market encompasses tools designed to assess the stability and integrity of structures and soil conditions. Extensometers measure ground movement or deformation, while piezometers monitor pore water pressures in soil or rock. Strain gauges are used to evaluate deformation in structures, and thermometers track temperature variations that could affect material properties. Additional devices may include inclinometers and load cells, contributing to comprehensive monitoring for engineering safety and performance.</p></p>
