<p><p>Metalized Coil CPP (Cast Polypropylene) Film is a type of packaging material that is coated with a thin layer of metal, typically aluminum, to enhance its barrier properties, cosmetic appeal, and durability. This film is widely used in the food, beverage, cosmetics, and pharmaceutical industries due to its excellent moisture, gas, and light barrier properties, which help extend the shelf life of products.</p><p>The Metalized Coil CPP Film Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.1% during the forecast period.
