<p><p>The flotation equipment market is segmented into three main types: pneumatic flotation machines, mechanical agitation flotation machines, and others. Pneumatic flotation machines utilize air to create bubbles for separating materials, offering efficiency in mineral processing. Mechanical agitation flotation machines rely on mechanical stirring to enhance the contact between air bubbles and particles, promoting separation. Other types encompass various advanced or specialized flotation technologies designed for unique applications, catering to diverse industries such as mining, recycling, and wastewater treatment.</p></p>
