<p><ul><li>Threaded Fasteners</li><li>Non-Threaded Fasteners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The **Truck Fastener Market** includes **Threaded Fasteners** and **Non-Threaded Fasteners**. Threaded fasteners, such as bolts, screws, and nuts, are designed with helical ridges to securely join components through rotational force, ensuring strong, adjustable connections. Non-threaded fasteners, like rivets, pins, and clips, rely on pressure, friction, or deformation for assembly, offering quick, permanent, or semi-permanent solutions. Both types are critical in truck manufacturing, providing durability, reliability, and structural integrity for various applications, from chassis assembly to interior fittings.</p></p>
