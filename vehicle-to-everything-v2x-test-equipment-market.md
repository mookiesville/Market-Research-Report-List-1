<p><p>The Vehicle-to-everything (V2X) test equipment market consists of two primary connectivity types: Dedicated Short-Range Communications (DSRC) and Cellular Connectivity. DSRC is a wireless communication technology tailored for short-range interactions, enabling vehicles to exchange information with each other and infrastructure, enhancing safety and traffic efficiency. On the other hand, Cellular Connectivity leverages existing telecommunications networks (like 4G and 5G) to enable broader data transmission, allowing vehicles to communicate over longer distances and access cloud-based services for improved mobility solutions.</p></p>
