<p><p>The disposable plastic closures market includes primarily two types: PE (Polyethylene) closures and PP (Polypropylene) closures. PE closures are lightweight, flexible, and are often used in beverage packaging due to their excellent sealing properties and resistance to moisture. In contrast, PP closures are more rigid and offer better thermal stability, making them suitable for applications requiring higher temperature resistance. Both types cater to various industries, including food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and consumer goods, ensuring product integrity and convenience.</p></p>
