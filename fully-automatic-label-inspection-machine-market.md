<p><p>The Fully Automatic Label Inspection Machine market is primarily divided into two types: Offline and Online. Offline machines operate independently from production lines, allowing for batch inspections after labeling, which is ideal for quality control prior to packaging. In contrast, Online machines are integrated directly into production lines, enabling real-time inspection as labels are applied, thus ensuring immediate detection of defects. Both types enhance quality assurance but serve different operational needs in manufacturing environments.</p></p>
