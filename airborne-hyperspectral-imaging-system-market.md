<p><p>The Airborne Hyperspectral Imaging System market includes various types categorized by wavelength ranges. Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR) systems excel in material identification and moisture detection. Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR) is used for thermal imaging and surveillance. Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR) is effective in analyzing heat signatures. Additional technologies like UV-Vis, FT-IR, and combined systems cater to specialized applications across multiple industries.</p></p>
