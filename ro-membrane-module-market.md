<p><p>The RO membrane module market is primarily categorized into two types: thin-film membranes and cellulose acetate membranes. Thin-film membranes are made from synthetic materials and offer higher salt rejection and durability, making them ideal for various applications. In contrast, cellulose acetate membranes are composed of natural materials and are less expensive but exhibit lower performance in terms of fouling resistance and longevity. Both types are essential in various industries, including water purification, desalination, and wastewater treatment.</p></p>
