<p><p>The metal powder atomization equipment market is segmented into three main types: gas atomization equipment, water atomization equipment, and other methods. Gas atomization utilizes high-pressure gas to disperse molten metal into fine particles, resulting in high-quality powders suitable for various applications. Water atomization employs high-pressure water jets for similar dispersion, often producing larger particle sizes. Other methods may include centrifugal or mechanical processes, catering to specific material characteristics and powder attributes based on industry requirements.</p></p>
