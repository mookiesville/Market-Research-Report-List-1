<p><p>The biotherapeutics market encompasses various methods for producing and assessing biologic drugs. Transfection involves introducing foreign DNA into cells to create therapeutics. Single cell cloning allows for the isolation of individual cells, with techniques like manual limited dilution cloning, FACS (Fluorescence-Activated Cell Sorting), and ClonePix facilitating this process. Manual limited dilution cloning offers simplicity but lower throughput, while FACS enables precise sorting based on specific cell characteristics. ClonePix automates image-based colony picking, enhancing efficiency. Other methods include microfluidics and soft agar cloning for diverse applications.</p></p>
