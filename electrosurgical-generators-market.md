<p><strong>The Electrosurgical Generators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopole</li><li>Bipolar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrosurgical generators market includes two primary types: monopolar and bipolar systems. Monopolar generators use a single active electrode to cut or coagulate tissue, with a return electrode placed on the patient’s skin, making them versatile for various procedures. In contrast, bipolar generators have two active electrodes in close proximity, allowing for precise tissue manipulation with minimal lateral thermal damage, making them ideal for delicate surgeries. Together, these technologies cater to diverse surgical needs and enhance procedural efficiency.</p></p>
