<p><p>The organic cocoa market consists of three primary product types: cocoa liquor, cocoa powder, and cocoa butter. Cocoa liquor is a liquid form produced from ground cocoa beans, serving as a base for chocolate products. Cocoa powder, derived from pressed cocoa solids, is often used in baking and beverages for its rich flavor. Cocoa butter, the fat extracted from cocoa beans, is essential for chocolate formulation and skincare products. Together, these components cater to the growing demand for organic and sustainably sourced ingredients.</p></p>
