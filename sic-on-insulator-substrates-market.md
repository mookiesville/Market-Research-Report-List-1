<p><ul><li>Semi-insulating SiC Substrates</li><li>Conductive SiC Substrates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>SiC-on-insulator substrates are categorized into two main types: semi-insulating SiC substrates and conductive SiC substrates. Semi-insulating SiC substrates possess high resistivity, making them ideal for high-frequency and high-power applications, as they can effectively isolate electronic components. In contrast, conductive SiC substrates have controlled conductivity, enabling enhanced charge carrier mobility, making them suitable for power devices and high-temperature applications. Both types play a crucial role in advancing semiconductor technology, particularly in industries such as automotive and telecommunications.</p></p>
