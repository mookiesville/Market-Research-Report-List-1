<p><p>The skin cancer market consists of several key types, including basal cell carcinoma, squamous cell carcinoma, and melanoma, along with other less common forms. Basal cell carcinoma is the most prevalent and typically grows slowly, often linked to prolonged sun exposure. Squamous cell carcinoma arises from the outer layer of skin and can be more aggressive if untreated. Melanoma is the deadliest form, originating in pigment-producing cells, and requires prompt treatment. The "others" category encompasses various rarer skin cancers.</p></p>
