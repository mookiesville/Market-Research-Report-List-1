<p><strong>The Gesture Recognition Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polygonal Imaging</li><li>Light Flying Time</li><li>Structured Light</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gesture recognition systems utilize different technologies to interpret human gestures for interaction. Polygonal imaging focuses on capturing gestures through a series of geometric shapes, enabling efficient tracking and recognition. Light flying time (LiDAR) measures the time taken for light to reflect off objects, allowing precise gesture detection in three-dimensional space. Structured light involves projecting predefined light patterns onto surfaces to identify shapes and movements, thus facilitating accurate gesture recognition. Each method offers unique strengths for various applications in human-computer interaction.</p></p>
