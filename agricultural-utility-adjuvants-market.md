<p><p>The agricultural utility adjuvants market comprises various product types that enhance the efficacy of pesticides and fertilizers. Compatibility agents ensure that different formulations mix well without degrading. Buffers and acidifiers adjust the pH of spray solutions to optimize chemical activity. Antifoam agents reduce foam formation during mixing and application, while water conditioners neutralize harmful minerals in water. Antidrift agents minimize spray drift, ensuring targeted application. Other utility adjuvants encompass a range of specialized formulations for various agricultural needs.</p></p>
