<p><p>The disposable blood lancet market can be categorized into two main types: safety blood collection needles and standard blood collection needles. Safety blood collection needles are designed with features that minimize the risk of needle-stick injuries, incorporating mechanisms that retract or cover the needle after use. Both types are widely used for blood sampling in medical and diagnostic settings.</p></p>
