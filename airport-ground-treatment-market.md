<p><ul><li>Sand Drain Method</li><li>PVD Method</li><li>Vibro Compaction Method,</li><li>Pneumatic Flow Mixing Method</li><li>Jet Grouting</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Airport Ground Treatment Market encompasses several methods for improving soil stability and load-bearing capacity. The Sand Drain Method involves inserting sand tubes to enhance drainage. The PVD Method employs prefabricated vertical drains for rapid consolidation. Vibro Compaction uses vibration to densify soil, improving stability. Pneumatic Flow Mixing Method injects cement and other materials into soil to enhance strength. Jet Grouting mixes grout with soil using high-pressure jets. Other methods may include techniques like soil stabilization and deep mixing, catering to specific site conditions.</p></p>
