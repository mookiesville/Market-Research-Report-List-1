<p><p>The virtual microscopy market encompasses various imaging types, notably brightfield and fluorescence microscopy. Brightfield microscopy provides high-resolution images using visible light, primarily for examining stained tissue samples. In contrast, fluorescence microscopy utilizes specific wavelengths of light to excite fluorophores, allowing for the visualization of cellular components and structures with enhanced contrast. Each type serves distinct applications in research and diagnostic pathology, addressing different imaging needs and facilitating advanced analysis in various scientific fields.</p></p>
