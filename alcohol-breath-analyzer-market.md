<p><p>The alcohol breath analyzer market comprises three primary types: fuel cell, semiconductor, and infrared. Fuel cell analyzers use electrochemical reactions to measure blood alcohol content, offering high accuracy and reliability. Semiconductor analyzers utilize metal oxide sensors to detect alcohol vapors, providing a cost-effective solution, though generally less precise. Infrared analyzers measure alcohol concentration by analyzing the absorption of infrared light at specific wavelengths, ensuring high sensitivity and accuracy, making them suitable for both professional and personal use.</p></p>
