<p><p>The parameter hematology analyzer market is segmented into various types, including laser type, electrical impedance type, capacitive type, and others. Laser type analyzers utilize laser light scattering to analyze cell size and structure, providing detailed information about blood components. Electrical impedance analyzers count cells based on resistance changes as cells pass through an aperture. Capacitive type analyzers measure changes in capacitance to detect cell characteristics. Other types may include methods like optical coherence and fluorescence, catering to diverse clinical needs.</p></p>
