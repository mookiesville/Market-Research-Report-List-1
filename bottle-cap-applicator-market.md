<p><p>The bottle cap applicator market is segmented into two primary types: printable and spray-on. Printable applicators allow brands to directly print labels or designs onto caps, enhancing customization and branding. This method offers precise control over aesthetics and information visibility. Spray-on applicators, on the other hand, facilitate the application of coatings or finishes directly onto caps, promoting efficiency in production and often providing added functionalities like tamper resistance or UV protection. Both types cater to varying consumer and branding needs.</p></p>
