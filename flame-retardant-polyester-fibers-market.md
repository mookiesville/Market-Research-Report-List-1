<p><p>The flame retardant polyester fibers market comprises two main types: flame retardant staple fibers and flame retardant filaments. Flame retardant staple fibers are short strands used in textiles and upholstery, providing enhanced fire resistance for a variety of applications. In contrast, flame retardant filaments are continuous strands, ideal for weaving and knitting high-performance fabrics. Both types serve critical roles in safety-focused industries, including automotive, aerospace, and construction, by reducing flammability and improving material durability.</p></p>
