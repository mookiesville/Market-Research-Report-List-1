<p><p>The micro heat pipe market comprises several specialized types designed for efficient thermal management. Pulsating heat pipes utilize a two-phase fluid that oscillates, enhancing heat transfer. Microgrooved plate heat pipes feature grooves that facilitate fluid movement, offering compact and efficient cooling solutions. Loop heat pipes, on the other hand, employ a capillary mechanism to circulate fluid, providing effective heat transport over long distances. Each type serves unique applications in electronics cooling, aerospace, and other industries requiring efficient thermal regulation.</p></p>
