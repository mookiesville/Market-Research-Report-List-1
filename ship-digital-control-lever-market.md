<p><p>The Ship Digital Control Lever market is divided into two primary types: multi-lever and single-lever systems. Multi-lever systems feature several independent levers, allowing precise control of multiple functions, such as throttle and gear selection, simultaneously. This configuration is ideal for larger vessels requiring intricate maneuvers. In contrast, single-lever systems consolidate control into one handle, enabling simplified operation, especially beneficial for smaller ships and new operators. Both types enhance the efficiency and safety of maritime navigation.</p></p>
