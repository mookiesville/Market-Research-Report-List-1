<p><p>Piezoceramic components in the automotive market primarily include single plate and multilayer piezo ceramics. Single plate piezo ceramics are simple, cost-effective solutions, suitable for applications requiring basic actuation and sensing capabilities. In contrast, multilayer piezo ceramics consist of multiple layers stacked together, providing higher performance and greater displacement in a more compact design, ideal for advanced applications like fuel injection systems, active suspensions, and adaptive control systems, enhancing vehicle efficiency and functionality.</p></p>
