<p><p>An induction toilet is an advanced sanitation system that utilizes electromagnetic induction technology to efficiently manage waste. Unlike traditional toilets, induction toilets use a magnetic field to heat and decompose waste, reducing it to ash or sterilized residue. This technology is highly energy-efficient, odor-free, and environmentally friendly, making it suitable for both residential and commercial applications. Induction toilets are particularly popular in regions facing water scarcity, as they require minimal to no water for operation.</p><p>The Induction Toilet Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13% during the forecast period.
